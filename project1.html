Work-Life Wellness Hub

Helping young professionals manage stress with a responsive, mood-enhancing journaling platform. Designed for emotional resonance and seamless usability using HTML/CSS/Bootstrap.

Here I will write a short explanation of the overall project, its goals, and the design choices made to enhance user experience.

Conclusion & Future Opportunities

Key Learnings

Empathetic design leads to better engagement. A soft visual language, paired with intuitive structure, improved emotional connection and user completion rates.

Next Iterations

  • Emotion-based recommendations using sentiment tracking
  • Modular journaling tools to personalize entries
  • Onboarding redesign to reduce bounce rate

User feedback surfaced confusion with icons and visual cues, leading to planned adjustments in visual hierarchy and interaction clarity in future sprints.
