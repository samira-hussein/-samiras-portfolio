Work-Life-Wellness-Hub

The Mental Health Awareness Webpage is a beginner-friendly, accessible platform designed to provide supportive information about mental health. The page offers users easy-to-understand insights on how to recognize common mental health issues, manage stress, and find mental health resources, all within a clean, calming layout.

Project Overview

This project focuses on creating a user-friendly interface for individuals seeking mental health resources.

Design Process

We conducted user research to understand the needs of our audience, followed by iterative design and testing phases.

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap were utilized to build a responsive and accessible webpage.
